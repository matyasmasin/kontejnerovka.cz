Quick facts

Equipment in one answer

Container delivery depends on truck access, safe standing place, material weight and whether the container can stand on private land or a public place. A photo of the access usually makes the quote faster.

  • Heavy materials: rubble, concrete and wet soil
  • Access issues: narrow driveways, slope, parked cars, branches, cables and soft ground
  • Public street placement may require local permission

Weight and volume

Container size is chosen by material, not just volume

Light bulky material is different from heavy rubble, concrete or wet soil. The safe solution depends on both volume and weight.

Rubble and concrete

Heavy material where clean sorting and safe weight matter.

Soil
